# What is Nekomancer?

Nekomancer is a **decentralized exchange (DEX) swap aggregator** built on **Monad Mainnet**. It automatically finds the best prices across multiple DEXes to give you the most tokens for your swap.

## Why Use Nekomancer?

### Best Prices
We route your trades through multiple DEXes including:
- Uniswap V2 & V3
- PancakeSwap V2
- Nad.Fun (for meme tokens)

Our pathfinder algorithm finds the optimal route, sometimes splitting large trades across multiple DEXes for better execution.

### Earn While You Trade
- **Referral Program**: Share your link and earn 20% of fees from everyone you refer
- **Staking Rewards**: Stake MANCER tokens to earn 50% of all platform fees

### Transparent Fees
- **1% Platform Fee** on each swap
- Fee split: 30% Platform, 20% Referrals, 50% Staking
- All fees visible on-chain

### Built on Monad
- Fast transactions (sub-second finality)
- Low gas costs
- EVM-compatible (works with MetaMask, OKX, etc.)
