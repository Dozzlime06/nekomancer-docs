# Get USDC

All trading on Nekomancer uses USDC. You need USDC on Monad to participate.

---

## USDC Contract

```
0x754704Bc059F8C67012fEd69BC8A327a5aafb603
```

This is the official USDC contract on Monad mainnet.

---

## Getting USDC

### Option 1: Bridge from Other Chains

Use official bridges to move USDC from Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, or other chains to Monad.

### Option 2: DEX Swap

Swap MON or other tokens for USDC on Monad DEXs.

### Option 3: CEX Withdrawal

Some exchanges support direct Monad withdrawals.

---

## Adding USDC to Wallet

If USDC doesn't show in your wallet:

1. Open your wallet
2. Click **Import Token**
3. Paste: `0x754704Bc059F8C67012fEd69BC8A327a5aafb603`
4. Symbol: USDC
5. Decimals: 6

---

## Minimum Amounts

No minimum to deposit. Trade with any amount.

Gas fees on Monad are paid in MON. Keep some MON for transactions.
